Bo Luc Lac (Vietnamese Shaking Beef)

Description

This Bo Luc Lac recipe is based off KT Foods Bo Luc Lac Recipe.

Ingredients

Marinade

  • 5 lbs beef
  • 3 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1 soup spoon oil
  • 3 soup spoons mixed sauce

Sauce

  • 1/4 cup oyster sauce
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Make sauce in ratios described under Ingredients.
  2. Cut beef into cubes.
  3. Marinade beef at least overnight but ideally up to 24 hours.
  4. When beef is ready, stir fry with onions and other desired sides.

Tips and Tricks

  • Need to try straining sauce from beef before cooking. Add the sauce back afterwards for more flavor.
  • Rewatch video to see if corn starch was an ingredient.
